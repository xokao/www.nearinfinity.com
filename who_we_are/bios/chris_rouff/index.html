Chris Rouff is the Managing Director of Contract Research and Development at Near Infinity. He recently joined the company in June of 2012 where he is developing and executing new CRAD business.

Prior to joining Near Infinity, Chris worked for Lockheed Martin, SAIC and NASA Goddard Spaceflight Center where he was involved in business development, program management and doing research in software engineering, agent-based and swarm-based systems.

Chris holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Southern California, a M.S. in Computer Science from the University of California, Davis, and a B.A. in Mathematics/Computer Science from the California State University, Fresno.
