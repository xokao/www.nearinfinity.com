Gary joined Near Infinity in 2006. Gary oversees the Company's day-to-day operations, human resources, contracts, cash management, financial reporting and budgeting, forecasting, and strategic planning.

Gary has over 23 years of experience in accounting and finance in both the public and private sectors. Prior to joining Near Infinity, Gary was the Corporate Controller at Reston, Virginia-based Learning Tree International, Inc. Before Learning Tree, Gary worked for the public accounting firms of Coopers & Lybrand in Los Angeles, CA and Ernst & Young in Pittsburgh, PA. At Coopers & Lybrand, Gary worked on federal, state and local tax issues as part of their corporate tax consulting group. At Ernst & Young, Gary worked on audit engagements as part of their audit and accounting service group. Gary was also a manager in the Corporate Tax Department of Manor Care, Inc. in Silver Spring, Maryland.

Gary holds a Masters of Business Administration from The George Washington University in Washington D.C. and a Bachelor of Arts in Accounting and Business Administration from Thiel College in Greenville, Pennsylvania. He is a CPA and a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and the Virginia Society of Certified Public Accountants.
