Benefits

Near Infinity offers an extensive set of benefits, including 100% paid individual and family healthcare and a base of four weeks vacation / sick leave. But the benefit that truely sets Near Infinity apart is our training and development program, NIC-U (Near Infinity Corporation University). The cornerstone of the NIC-U program is a annual 6-day, $5,000 employee self-managed technical training and conference budget. We also offer all employees a yearly $500 book and software allowance, monthly in-house tech talks and a bi-annual in-house technical conference. The company also hosts usergroup meet-ups and code competitions. See below for a complete listing of our benefits:

Compensation & Retirement

  • Competitive salaries
  • Performance-based bonuses
  • 4 weeks of combined vacation/sick leave
  • 10 deferrable holidays
  • Performance-based stock option plan
  • Profit-sharing plan (discretionary)
  • Safe Harbor 401K with 4% company match
  • Employee Referral Bonus

  • Health and Dependent Flexible Spending plan

Training and Resources

  • Generous budget for books, software and membership fees
