Internship Program

Near Infinity is looking for talented students who love technology as much as we do. We accept resume submissions from undergraduate and graduate students in a wide range of computer science and engineering fields.

As a student in our program, you will work in a fun and challenging instructional environment on a variety of software design projects. As an intern, you can expect to learn something new every day.

We have two internship sessions annually, so please check out our current openings.
