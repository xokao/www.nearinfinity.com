Software Engineer

Near Infinity Corporation is currently seeking software engineers who share our passion for technology. If you desire to keep on top of technical trends while establishing a solid career within a growing organization, then this could be just the place for you.

WHAT WE LOOK FOR

Application developers/software engineers with demonstrated experience creating dynamic, highly interactive web-based applications using Ruby/Rails or Java EE and open source technologies, such as Spring, Hibernate, Ant and Maven.

Experience with client technologies such as CSS, JavaScript and AJAX (JQuery, Ext-JS, Prototype, Scriptaculous, etc.), and experience accessing relational database management systems, such as Oracle and MySQL. A solid understanding of n-tiered application architecture and enterprise design patterns is essential.

Additional "nice to haves":

  • Experience with Big Data (Hadoop, Hive, Lucene), Mobile (iOS, Android), and / or .NET (C#, LINQ)
  • Experience working in an Agile development environment
