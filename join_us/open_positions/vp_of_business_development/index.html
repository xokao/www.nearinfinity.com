VP of Business Development

The VP of Business Development will work closely with the President and other member of the executive leadership team to establish and maintain a business development strategy for the company building upon an excellent record of past performance for government and commercial clients.

POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Identify potential contracts and develop the tactics and teams needed to bring them to fruition
  • Develop and maintain strong business relationships with key decision makers in IC/DoD community and commercial markets
  • Spearhead business development initiatives that are consistent with the company’s overall strategy
  • Identify and qualify business opportunities with a focus on prime contracts and strategic sub-contracting opportunities
  • Develop teaming relationships, analyze the competition, develop win strategies, and track targeted opportunities
  • Qualify targeted opportunities so they can be transitioned to capture management and winning proposals
  • Conduct sales calls to targeted customers, present management and technical capabilities, convey a solid technical understanding of project/technical areas of interest, and identify requirements, win themes, and potential solutions for opportunities.
  • Develop a strong understanding of potential customer needs and expectations through direct contact to establish trusted relationships with executives, stakeholders, and decision makers
  • Promote services and solution offerings via direct contact, meetings, demonstrations, concept/white papers, industry conferences, and events
  • Facilitate prospective customers' understanding of the company’s core capabilities and help shape their perceptions of how service and solution offerings will meet their needs
  • Proactively develop new leads and contacts via direct prospecting into targeted accounts and build a pipeline that will yield short, mid, and long-term growth objectives
  • Actively lead the business development function in an effective, aggressive manner.
  • Understand and contribute to the company's vision by incorporating it into the BD strategy

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE

  • A proven record of success in sales and business development activities for an IT services and solutions company focused on defense and intelligence agencies, preferably at small to mid-sized, growth-oriented professional services firms
  • Demonstrated experience in performing all aspects of the BD life cycle, including strategic market assessment, new account development and management, capture management and proposal management
  • Experience in identifying/developing pre-RFP opportunities with prospective customers, analyzing RFP’s, analyzing competition, determining Bid/No Bid decisions, developing win strategy and win themes, ensuring RFP compliance and enhancing the win probability
  • A familiarity with the inner workings of relevant agencies, including solid current relationships with executive decision makers
  • Knowledge of and strong contacts within the systems integrator community to facilitate the development of strategic teaming relationships
  • Demonstrated experience in account planning and strategy implementation.
  • Track record of winning new Federal contracts and acquiring new clients
  • Experience analyzing the competition, developing win themes, and enhancing the win probability of opportunities
  • Ability to convert large amounts of technical information into concise descriptions tailored to client business/operational needs
  • Thorough understanding of FAR and Government budget process
  • Demonstrated ability to tailor service offerings to market organizational strengths to senior level executives and operational leaders successfully
  • Strong understanding of technical concepts to include agile methodologies, software best practices, and cutting-edge technologies
  • B.S. or B.A. degree

SPECIALIZED SKILLS AND KNOWLEDGE

  • Energetic, forward-thinking and creative individual with high ethical standards and professional image
  • Well-organized and self-directed individual; ability to balance and prioritize competing requirements
  • Articulate individual who can relate to people at all levels of management and who possesses excellent written and oral presentation and communication skills
  • A team player; trustworthy and willing to share information with team members i n order to facilitate a collaborative environment
  • Active TS/SCI or higher security clearance
