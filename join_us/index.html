GET TO KNOW US

Founded in 2002, Near Infinity is a software development firm based in Reston, Virginia. Our 10 years of strong growth and financial performance together with our close-knit, people-centric culture make Near Infinity the perfect blend of small and large company attributes.

Use Your Skills

In selecting projects, we look for challenging, mission-focused work where our teams can make a real difference.

Tech Focus

NIC people love technology, and we love to learn. Our extensive training program includes an employee self-managed training budget of $5,000 and six days of paid time off for technical training.

People First

Near Infinity is all about people, and it shows with our less then 10% turn-over rate. Employees stay because of the great benefits, close-knit culture, and interesting and rewarding work.

Work = Fun
