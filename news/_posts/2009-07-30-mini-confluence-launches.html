NIC Labs launched Mini Confluence, an iPhone version of Atlassian's popular wiki, Confluence. Mini Confluence's features include: the dashboard, which allows users to quickly scan recent news, comments and pages; viewing and posting comments; full text searching; viewing of PDF, Word, and image attachments; people view with searching, browsing by space; and internal and external linking.

Mini Confluence is available now in the iPhone app store. {% endraw %}
